Before Visiting Kolkata

Important Information

Before visiting Kolkata, there are several things you should consider. The weather can be quite humid, especially during the summer months. Be sure to pack light clothing and stay hydrated.

Kolkata has a rich cultural heritage. While traveling, respect local customs and traditions. The city is known for its festivals, such as Durga Puja, and these can impact traffic and transportation.

Public transportation in Kolkata includes buses, trams, and the metro. You can also find iconic yellow taxis everywhere. Plan your travel accordingly to avoid peak hours.
