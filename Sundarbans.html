Information Before Visiting Sundarbans

Important Information

The Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the largest mangrove forest in the world and home to the majestic Royal Bengal Tigers. Located in West Bengal, it offers a unique blend of wildlife and natural beauty. The best time to visit is between November and February, when the weather is pleasant, and wildlife sightings are frequent. Travelers need to obtain permits from the Forest Department for entry and boat rides. The journey typically starts from Kolkata, followed by a road trip to Canning or Godkhali, where boats take visitors into the forest. For accommodation, eco-resorts, homestays, and houseboats provide an immersive experience. Visitors should respect the delicate ecosystem by following eco-friendly practices, hiring local guides, and enjoying the local culture and cuisine responsibly.
